About

RDZ / About

RDZ or REINE DES ZIBANS ,established since July 2021, is the first pioneering force in aluminum monobloc aerosol cans manufacturing in North Africa , and a leading company specializing in the production of high-quality, single-piece aluminum aerosol bottles for the cosmetic and other industries. The company guarantees compliance with strict safety and hygiene standards. RDZ offers a range of aerosol bottles in different sizes to meet the specific needs of each client, and also provides consultation services to find innovative solutions that meet the most demanding requirements of the cosmetic industry. By working closely with its clients, RDZ ensures that the products it manufactures meet their needs and expectations perfectly.

What printing techniques do you use for aerosol cans?

We use high-quality dry offset printing, capable of applying up to 9 PANTONE colors, ensuring vibrant designs and a wide range of customization possibilities.

Can you produce customized products?

Yes, we offer a wide range of customization options. You can choose from various bottle shapes and heights, with two available diameters: 45mm and 53mm. Additionally, we provide customization for color, and surface treatment. Our surface treatments include glossy, semi-matte, and soft-touch finishes, providing different tactile and visual effects to match your brandâ€™s style.

Do you support CMYK color printing for branding?

No, we do not support CMYK printing. We exclusively use Pantone color matching and are capable of applying up to 9 pantone colors.

What is the MOQ (Minimum Order Quantity) for your products?

Our minimum order quantity (MOQ) is 20,000 units per product.

What industries can benefit from RDZ aluminum aerosol cans?

Our aerosol cans are widely used in the cosmetic, pharmaceutical, personal care, household, and industrial sectors, offering safe and reliable packaging solutions.

Does the internal varnish affect products with strong cleaning agents?

No, the internal varnish does not affect products with strong cleaning agents, shaving foam, pharmaceutical products, or any other similar substances (aggressive ingredients). It is designed to protect the product and ensure no interaction with the contents, ensuring everything functions perfectly.

What are the procedures to follow when working with you?

Contact us, request a quote, approve designs, and we'll begin production.
